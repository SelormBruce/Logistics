Who we are

Fradal Logistics At A Glance

Is a multi stream business service organisation, set up to facilitate trade by providing much needed intermediary service to sector operators. Fradal logistics primarily specialises in supply chain management, including sourcing, procurement, multimodal transportation, material handling, freight forwarding, haulage, haulage to distribution.We provide consultancy services in all areas of international trade and logistics and other related activities.
